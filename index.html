About Me

Nnedimma Ugochukwu is currently a senior at the University of Pittsburgh at Bradford majoring in Computer Information Systems and Technology (CIST) with a minor in Digital Graphic Design. She aspires to travel the world, take photos, and design neat things.

After graduation in 2018, she plans to continue her travel adventures by spanning across Europe and Asia to see more than ever before. Along with that, she will continue to perfect her Chinese and take on more fulfilling activities.
