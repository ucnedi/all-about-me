More About Nnedimma...

Nnedimma Ugochukwu is currently a junior at the University of Pittsburgh at Bradford majoring in Computer Information Systems and Technology (CIST) with a minor in Digital Graphic Design aspiring to one day help many others in their day to day lives.

During her free time, she loves to go biking, write postcards, read manga, play the piano, take photos, and most importantly, travel. In the past year, she studied abroad and has visited over 7 countries and has plans for much much more.
