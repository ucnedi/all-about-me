About Me

Nnedimma Ugochukwu is currently a senior at the University of Pittsburgh at Bradford majoring in Computer Information Systems and Technology (CIST) with a minor in Digital Graphic Design. She aspires to one day change the world one day at a time.

After graduation in 2018, she aspires to continue her travel adventures by spanning across Europe and Asia to see more than before. Along with that, possibly even pick up another language along the way.
